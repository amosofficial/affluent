About Us

Affluent Farmers is a startup Agro-Consultancy and Agro-Input Supplies firm based in Nairobi, Kenya.

AF has been in operation since the year 2018. We started as a social media platform aimed at empowering youth and women groups by equipping them with information on modern farming techniques and feasible agribusiness projects.

over time, we have become a blessing to many farmers through our model of providing credible, reliable,comprehensive,and up-to-date agribusiness information.

Many prospects have in the past benefited,still benefit today,and will in future,continue reaping from the invaluable information,high-quality farms inputs,crop varieties,and animal breeds that we offer them.

We are also strong advocates and ambassadors of environmental conversation and climate change mitigation. Our business is guided by the United Nations (UN) Sustainable Development Goals, which enable us to tackle the most serious issues affecting our society through sustainable agriculture.

Our story:Why we exist today

Affluent Farmers is an Agro-Consultancy and Agro-Supplies Firm located in Nairobi, Kenya.Affluent Farmers, AF, started as a Facebook Page intended to offer farmers the most credible,reliable, comprehensive, and up-to-date agriculture information. The effort was aimed atbridging the agricultural knowledge gap that hindered farmers and potential farmers fromachieving their maximum potential.

We dubbed our page Affluent Farmers to illustrate the fact that, unlike a common perception,mostly associating agriculture with the poor or the peasants, farming can, actually, be a source ofaffluence.

In so doing, we mainly targets the youth who, the society have for a long time, erroneously,made believe that farming is for the elderly. Similarly, we focus on women groups, whomimplications of gender disparities and the patriarchal society nature disadvantages, leaving themunemployed, without enough farming land, and totally incapable of fending their families.

Affluent Farmers, therefore, intended to come up with a solution to the problems facing theyouth and women groups, mainly through creating awareness on the importance of adoptingmodern agricultural techniques and feasible agricultural projects.

As a result, Affluent Farmers became selective, only picking agribusiness projects that we feltare most suitable and/or viable for the youth and women regardless of their employment status,size of land owned, geographical location, education level, among other aspects that couldimplicate on farming outcomes.

For example, we promote the adoption of ventures like Mushrooms and Rabbit farming. Theseprojects, among the others that Affluent Farmers has chosen, require low starting capital on asmall scale, relatively little spaces/ land, do well both in rural and urban areas including indoors,have unmet market demands, among other factors that make them viable for small scale farmerswho wish to start small and grow.

Following months of sharing agricultural information, we realized the interest and great impactthat we had on our audience. This pushed and encouraged Affluent Farmers to start offeringconsultancy services to interested farmers as a way of bringing a one on one aspect of theinformation sharing idea. Consequently, a business idea was hatched. Notably, not an ordinarybusiness but, one aimed at empowering and building capacities of the youth and women groups,generally marginalized in the society.

As we grew, prospects became more interested, requesting for farm inputs on the ventures in ourscope of operation. The demand prompted us to work with like-minded agripreneurs to help meetthe farmers' needs for high-quality farm inputs, crop varieties, and animal breeds. AffluentFarmers' endeavor is so far a success with many people in Kenya, the East Africa region, andbeyond benefiting from our services.

As we grow, our objectives are to continue sharing comprehensive agricultural information to allwithout limit or holding back. At AF we believe that information is power, and that with genuineagribusiness information, we can grow a sustainable society that is food secure, healthy, and thatenjoys a considerable standard of living.

To promote the poor rural farmers, we provide absolutely free consultancy services to registeredfarmers groups. This is in an effort to, also, encourage other farmers to unite and have a single,strong voice of a multitude that not only enhances their bargaining power in buying farm inputsbut also in marketing their produce, by easily eliminating the middle-men.

In the same spirit, we continue sharing information free of charge to all our audience, throughour social media platforms like Facebook and Twitter. We also share modern farming guidelines,news, trends, and insights on our website, in a variety of areas not only covering farming but alsohuman and environmental health. We are strong advocates of environmental conservation andpractices aimed at mitigating climate change.

Our consultancy services are affordable to ensure that our clients find no stumbling block;through a hefty price for information, to realize their agribusiness goals. The fees charged in ourindividual clients' training courses, alongside the earnings from agro-input supplies helps sustainthe AF business.

Also, to keep growing, and to benefit more needy farmers, we encourage well-wishers to keepsponsoring our projects for a good course. Our aims, are not to make more money, but to upliftunderprivileged, but able farmers be it the youth, elderly women or men. Noteworthy, while ourfocus in on the youth and women groups, we never discriminate, but offer our services equally toall in need.

Certainly, you now understand why we exist and how we will continue collaborating.Remember, at Affluent Farmers, we aim at helping all to see far in Agribusiness.
